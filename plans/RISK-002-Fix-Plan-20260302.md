## Executive Summary

What began as an investigation into RISK-002's failure revealed a systemic fragility in how VAPT Secure handles enforcement when implementation data is missing or incomplete in the database. While the Hook driver had a localized fix for this, other drivers (Htaccess, Nginx, IIS, Caddy, Config) were skipping rule generation entirely.

**Objective:** Standardize and centralize the "Effective Data Resolution" logic across the entire plugin to ensure consistent protection.
