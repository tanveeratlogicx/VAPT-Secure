Lack of Rate Limiting on WordPress Login

A07:2025 CWE-307 Authentication

wp-login.php lacks brute-force protection, enabling unlimited login attempts. An attacker can run automated credential stuffing or dictionary attacks with no lockout mechanism in place.

⚠ ATTACK SCENARIO

Attacker launches automated brute-force or credential stuffing attack against /wp-login.php — thousands of attempts per minute with no IP lockout, CAPTCHA, or rate limiting to stop them.

TEST PAYLOADS
POST /wp-login.php → ip_blocked after 5 attempts ◉ manual
✦ REMEDIATION

Deploy fail2ban with WordPress jail config. Add Nginx limit_req_zone directive. Lock out IP after 5 failed attempts within 10 minutes. Consider Cloudflare Turnstile or reCAPTCHA v3 as secondary layer.
